Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Voice Vigil

How exactly does Voice Vigil work?

We redirect your loved one's phone calls through our secure system. When a call comes in, our AI monitors the conversation for scam patterns and suspicious requests. You'll receive real-time alerts for any concerning conversations and can join the call immediately if needed.

Does my loved one need a special phone or device?

No. Voice Vigil works with any existing landline or mobile phone. There's nothing to install and no new devices to learn how to use. We handle all the technical aspects behind the scenes.

Will my loved one keep their existing phone number?

Yes, they will keep their existing phone number. There's no disruption to their regular routine or need to update contact information with friends and family.

How quickly will I be notified about suspicious calls?

Notifications are sent in real-time as soon as our system detects potential scam indicators. You'll receive an immediate alert with details about the potential threat and options to take action.

Is there a limit to how many family members can receive alerts?

Our standard plan allows up to 5 family guardians to receive alerts and have access to the monitoring account. Additional guardians can be added for a small monthly fee.

What happens to the call recordings?

Call recordings are stored securely for 30 days and then automatically deleted unless you choose to save them longer. Only authorized family guardians can access the recordings through your secure account.
